Introduction

Inspired by the natural phenomena of cloths and liquids, Ricardo Sanchez explores spring mesh dynamics using box2D physics. SpringMesh is an interactive cloth you can twist, pull and deform using multitouch or gravity.

Physics Settings

Adjust physics parameters like, spring elasticity, switch touch attraction, force radius, gravity and mesh density.

Mesh Settings

Change mesh appearance, adjust color and switch between render modes including fills, wireframe and points.

Randomize

Apply random to slider values. Color, mesh density, spring damping and frequency are randomised.
